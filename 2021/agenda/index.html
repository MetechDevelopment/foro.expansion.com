Yanis VAROUFAKIS

Economista y exministro de Finanzas de Grecia

An economist and politician, he is Professor of Economic Theory at the University of Athens and, since 2018, Secretary General of the political party European Realistic Disobedience Front (MeRA25).

Mário CENTENO

Gobernador del Banco de Portugal

Mário Centeno has been Governor of Banco de Portugal since 20 July 2020. Prior to that, he was Portugal’s Minister of Finance from 2015 to 2020 and Eurogroup’s President from 2018 to 2020. He also represented the Euro Area at the G7 Finance Ministers group and at the G4 Finance Ministers group. He was also a member of the Five Presidents of the European Union group, where he took part in setting an European strategy to deal with the health and economic crisis in 2020. During his term as Minister of Finance, Portugal registered its first public accounts surplus during the current democratic period. As part of his duties as Minister of Finance, he also represented Portugal as Governor of the European Stability Mechanism, the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, the European Investment Bank, the African Development Bank Group, the Asian Development Bank, the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank, the Inter-American Development Bank Group, the Development Bank of Latin America, the Common Fund for Commodities and the World Bank Group.

From 2004 to 2013, he was Deputy Head of the Economics and Research Department of Banco de Portugal and, from 2014 to 2015, Advisor to the Board of Directors of the Banco de Portugal. During this period, he was also a member of the Statistical Council and the Economic Policy Committee of the European Commission. He received his PhD in Economics from Harvard University in 2000.
