El Anfitrión Perfecto

Corrado PASSERA

After graduating in Business Administration from Bocconi University, obtaining an MBA from the Wharton School in Philadelphia and his first professional years at McKinsey, he based his professional career on the challenge of innovation: from Olivetti to illimity, passing through the Poste, Intesa Sanpaolo and the position of Government.

In Olivetti the challenge was the transition from traditional information technology to ICT, which led, among other things, to the creation of two very successful startups such as Omnitel and Infostrada.

At Poste Italiane, between 1998 and 2002, he managed to transform an inefficient public body into a modern postal operator and one of the most successful postal banks in the world. It was during this period that he created Poste Vita, which in just a few years achieved national leadership. In the banking sector, first as CEO of Ambroveneto, then of Banca Intesa and finally of Intesa Sanpaolo, he helped create one of the most solid banks in Europe.

Among the start-ups of this period, it is worth mentioning Banca Prossima, a bank dedicated to social enterprise and impact finance, which is still one of the most effective examples at international level. As Minister of Economic Development, he introduced particularly startup-friendly legislation and deeply reorganised the world of innovation incentives.

In 2018, he created a banking startup through SPAXS, the largest special purpose acquisition company (SPAC) launched in Europe, raising €600 million. The aim was to create a "new paradigm bank" specialising in hard credit for SMEs and digital and mobile banking services to businesses and households.

The new illimity bank, of which he is CEO, has been listed on the STAR segment of the Italian Stock Exchange since 10 September 2020.
